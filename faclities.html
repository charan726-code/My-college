Marwadi University (MU) in Rajkot features a sprawling 54-acre campus equipped with extensive, modern facilities. The university offers separate, well-equipped gymnasiums for boys and girls, alongside a 4-acre sports complex, a lake with a jogging track, and various other recreational and academic amenities



A complete breakdown of the fitness, sports, and campus facilities includes:

Fitness & Sports

FacilitiesGymnasium: Fully equipped separate indoor gyms for boys and girls.





Outdoor Courts & Fields: Facilities for Cricket, Hockey, Soccer/Football, Volleyball, Basketball, and Tennis. Athletic Track: Standard athletics track for field and track events (running, long/high jump, shot put, javelin, and discus throw).
Indoor Sports: Dedicated indoor areas for Table Tennis, Chess, and Carrom.
Jogging Track: A scenic walking and jogging track situated around the campus lake.



Campus & Hostel AmenitiesHostel Accommodations:
Shared, fully furnished rooms with Wi-Fi, 24/7 security, and attached mess providing four meals a day.
Free Laundry Services: Includes daily washing of clothes, weekly towels, and periodic bedsheet washing for hostel residents.





Library: Digitized library featuring Radio Frequency Identification Device (RFID) technology, comprehensive book collections, and e-library access.




Healthcare: A 24-hour medical room and ambulance service.
Food & Dining: A central cafeteria, food plaza, and South/North Indian canteens located within the hostel blocks and auditorium



The Marwadi University Auditorium is a premier indoor event facility located inside the Marwadi University campus on the Rajkot-Morbi Highway in Rajkot, Gujarat. It serves as a central hub for major academic, corporate, and cultural gatherings.




Banking & Transport: On-campus banking, ATM facilities, and a fleet of over 60 buses for transportation across Rajkot.
Cultural & Leisure: Dance rooms, music rooms, and a large amphitheatre with a seating capacity of over 1,000 spectators
