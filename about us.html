Welcome Marwadi university!

Marwadi University, located in Rajkot, Gujarat, is a NAAC A+ accredited private university recognized for academic excellence, industry-aligned programs, and strong placement support.



History and Accreditation:

Marwadi University (MU) was established in 2016 by the Marwadi Education Foundation Group and is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and approved by AICTE. It has received NAAC A+ accreditation, making it the only private university in Gujarat with this distinction, and has been recognized as a "Centre of Excellence" by the Government of Gujarat marwadiuniversity.ac.in. The university ranks among the top institutions in the state and is listed in NIRF 2025 rankings for universities (101-150) and engineering (201-300).
