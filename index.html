Welcome to Marwadi University!


We are a premier institution dedicated to providing quality education and research opportunities.
we offer a wide range of programs and services to support our students and faculty.

Marwadi University (MU) is a premier private university established in 2016 and located on a 52-acre campus in Rajkot, Gujarat. The institution is recognized as a "Centre of Excellence" by the Government of Gujarat and holds a prestigious NAAC A+ accreditation. Known for its multicultural student base, it hosts over 12,000 students from more than 50 countries.
